Services

At Bugs Or Us PR, we provide professional pest control services in Puerto Rico for homes and businesses. We handle common situations and more complex cases through inspection, targeted treatments, and preventive recommendations to help reduce the risk of reinfestation.

We provide service in Corozal and nearby municipalities such as Bayamón, Dorado, Manatí, Morovis, Naranjito, Toa Alta, Toa Baja, Vega Alta, and Vega Baja, with responsible attention and a focus on the safety of people, pets, and the environment.

Inspection and Estimates

  • Professional inspection of the property.
  • Identification of conditions that favor pests.
  • Free estimates depending on the requested service.

Residential and Commercial Services

  • Pest control for homes.
  • Pest control for businesses and commercial properties.
  • Interior and exterior treatments.

Pests We Handle

  • Cockroach control.
  • Ant control.
  • Control of rats and other rodents.
  • Fly control.
  • Mosquito control.
  • Flea and tick control.
  • Spider control.
  • Scorpion control and other common structural pests.

Preventive Maintenance

  • Monthly or periodic maintenance programs.
  • Preventive treatments indoors and outdoors.
  • Recommendations to reduce the risk of reinfestation.

Each service is evaluated based on the type of pest, the level of activity, and the conditions of the property. The goal is to apply the proper treatment and guide the customer on preventive measures that help keep the situation under control.
