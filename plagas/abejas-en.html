Bees

Description

Bees are essential pollinating insects for the environment and agriculture. Although they are usually peaceful, they may defend their hive if they feel threatened, especially when a swarm is settling near homes or businesses.

Are they dangerous?

  • A sting can cause pain, swelling, and redness.
  • In allergic individuals, the reaction can be severe and requires immediate medical attention.
  • When a hive or swarm is located in a walkway or active area, the risk increases for children and pets.

Common signs

  • Constant bee activity entering and leaving the same spot.
  • A swarm clustered on a branch, wall, eave, or structure.
  • Loud, persistent buzzing near roofs or cavities.

Prevention

  • Seal openings or cavities in structures (eaves, fascia, soffit, walls).
  • Check roof areas, sheds, and outdoor equipment where bees may nest.
  • Do not strike or spray a swarm without a professional evaluation.

Responsible management

Bees are important to the ecosystem. Before taking action, the situation should be evaluated to determine the safest and most appropriate management approach.
