Scorpion

Description

Scorpions are nocturnal arthropods that hide during the day in cracks, wood piles, debris, stones, and dark areas. In Puerto Rico, they may appear outdoors or indoors, especially in humid locations.

Risks

  • Painful sting that may cause swelling and burning.
  • Stronger reactions in children and in sensitive or allergic individuals.
  • Greater concern when activity occurs inside the home.

Why do they appear?

  • High humidity and poor ventilation.
  • Accumulated wood, debris, or stones near the structure.
  • Cracks and openings in walls, doors, windows, or floors.
  • Available food source such as other insects on the property.

Prevention

  • Seal cracks and entries around doors and windows.
  • Reduce humidity by fixing leaks and improving ventilation.
  • Keep yards clean and free of clutter.
  • Check shoes, clothing, and dark areas if there have been sightings.
