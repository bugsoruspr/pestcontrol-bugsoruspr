Rice Weevil

Description

The rice weevil is a stored-product pest. It attacks grains such as rice, corn, and other cereals, and it can infest pantries and storage areas when favorable conditions are present.

Why is it a problem?

  • It contaminates food with damaged grains, dust, and debris.
  • It reproduces and spreads quickly if not controlled.
  • It can go unnoticed until the infestation becomes large.

Common signs

  • Small insects near the pantry or inside bags and containers.
  • Grains with holes or visible damage.
  • Fine powder inside the package.

Prevention

  • Store rice and grains in airtight containers.
  • Inspect new products before placing them in storage.
  • Clean shelves and remove crumbs or food dust.
  • Rotate inventory on a first-in, first-out basis.
