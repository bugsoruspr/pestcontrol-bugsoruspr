Bats

Description

Bats play an important role in the ecosystem by helping control insects and participating in pollination. However, they can become a problem when they roost in roofs, attics, eaves, ceiling voids, or other parts of a home or business structure.

Risks and problems

  • Accumulation of guano and strong odors.
  • Staining and deterioration in roosting areas.
  • Sanitation concerns from contact with accumulated droppings.
  • Noise disturbances during nighttime hours.

Common signs

  • Persistent strong odor in the roof or attic.
  • Guano on the floor, walls, or entry areas.
  • Noises at night in the roof or high areas.
  • Sightings at dusk coming out from the same point.

Professional management (non-lethal)

  • Responsible management is based on structural exclusion: preventing access, allowing bats to exit safely, and properly sealing entry points.
  • This process requires careful evaluation to avoid trapping bats inside the structure.
