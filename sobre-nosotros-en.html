About Bugs Or Us PR

Javier Ortiz

Licensed Exterminator in Puerto Rico

Bugs Or Us PR is a local company founded in 2018 by licensed exterminator Javier Ortiz. The company is dedicated to professional pest control in Puerto Rico, offering services for homes and businesses through responsible solutions and safe pest management practices.

Every pest situation is different. That is why we begin with an inspection to identify the conditions that allow pests to be present. From there, targeted treatments and preventive recommendations are applied to reduce the risk of reinfestation.

Why choose us?

  • Licensed exterminator in Puerto Rico: service performed in compliance with applicable regulations.
  • Professional inspection: evaluation of the property to identify conditions that favor the pest.
  • Targeted treatments: solutions focused on the specific problem, with an emphasis on prevention.
  • Responsible service: priority on the safety of your family, pets, and the environment.
  • Local service: assistance for homes and businesses in Corozal and nearby municipalities.

License and Certifications

Services are provided by a licensed exterminator in Puerto Rico, in compliance with applicable regulations.

  • Exterminator License #7713

The license is issued by the Puerto Rico Department of Health – Environmental Health Division.

  • Department of Agriculture Certification #2018-C-14143

The Department of Agriculture certification includes the following categories:

  • Category 4: Pest control in ornamentals and turf.
  • Category 8A: Structural pest control.
  • Category 8C: Termite and moth management.

In addition, exterminators must complete continuing education courses to keep their licenses active and update their knowledge on safe pest management, safety, and the proper use of products.

Service Areas

Bugs Or Us PR serves homes and businesses in several municipalities in northern and central Puerto Rico, including:

Bayamón, Corozal, Dorado, Manatí, Morovis, Naranjito, Toa Alta, Toa Baja, Vega Alta, and Vega Baja.
